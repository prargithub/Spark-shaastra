About Us:

Spark is a national platform where students compete with each other across technical quizzes, case studies, and presentations, and in the process improve themselves.

In pursuit of turning our beliefs of knowledge sharing and encouraging scientific temperament into reality, Shaastra has been conducting quizzes and conferences for school students under the banner of Spark for over 10 years.
